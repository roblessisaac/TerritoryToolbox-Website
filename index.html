Tool: Territory Analysis

This tool cross-references your territory boundaries (KML file) with 911 Dispatch GIS points to give you an exact count of every deliverable address within your borders. Follow the simple steps below to process your map. This tool currently only works for territories within Milwaukee county, WI

Step 01

Download your KML

Export your territory boundaries from your mapping software as a standard .KML file.

Step 02

Upload to the Engine

Click the launch button to upload your file. To handle heavy geospatial processing, the analysis tool opens in a separate, dedicated window. The webpage may take a moment to load.

Step 03

Export your Excel file

Once the audit is complete, download your generated Analysis Excel spreadsheet containing your precise insights.

KML

Keyhole Markup Language (KML) is an XML-based file format used to display geographic boundaries, polygons, and point data in mapping engines like Google Earth and localized GIS packages.
Under your congregation settings or maps setup menu, find your digital boundary maps. Locate the "Export" configurations and choose the **KML** polygon format rather than JPEG imagery or PDF files.
You can construct boundary layouts free inside **Google My Maps** (google.com/mymaps). Simply draw polygon vectors around target neighborhoods, name them to align with your territory list, click the layer settings dots, and download the KML export.

What practical ways can you use your territory analysis?

Use Cases

There are many ways to utilize your territory analysis far beyond a basic spreadsheet report. Below are a few of the most effective strategies for turning raw address data into organized, ready-to-use resources that directly support the friends in their ministry.

Divide Large Territories

Avoid the guesswork of manually counting blocks. When the Analyzer engine reads your territory polygons, the engine instantly calculates exact address counts per territory. This allows you to make informed decisions on how to divide up territories since you understand their density. Every hall is different.

See Every Address

Save hours of manual mapping. Rather than physically scouting streets for hidden address ranges, you get an immediate overview of every home within your territory boundaries. The engine instantly maps out every valid delivery location, providing a clean dataset that is structured and ready to import directly into your management software.

Accurate and Thorough Maps

Generate incredibly reliable lists for letter writing and door-to-door ministry. The tool isolates side-by-side duplexes and nested apartment units that typical routing software skips over, ensuring publishers have reliable records to work with. Since the data is based on address points, and not tax records, many hard to identify units are revealed.

Powerful Filters

The beauty of a structured spreadsheet lies in its customization. Alongside built-in features, you have complete freedom to apply your own data filters. By utilizing the precise column parameters, you can sort your data by practically any metric down to the territory.
